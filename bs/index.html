SAFETY FOR YOU

To stay safe on the road, always be aware of other drivers and their behavior. Follow all traffic rules and stay in the flow of traffic to protect yourself and others. To prevent fatigue, take regular breaks on long drives and stay alert. If you’re driving an older or first car, consider using a dash cam to record any incidents or illegal activity for added security.

SAFETY FOR OTHERS

To be safe to others, you must ensure you don’t speed, cut lanes, or use the right lane when not overtaking. This helps keep others safe and prevents fines. To avoid fatigue, take regular breaks and stay alert while driving.
